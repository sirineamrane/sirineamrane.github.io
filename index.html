More about me

Hi, I'm Sirine — a Computer Science student with a deep focus on Data Science, currently completing my BSc at the University of Hertfordshire.

I'm drawn to the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and real-world problem-solving. For me, data isn't just numbers — it's stories waiting to be told, patterns waiting to be found, and decisions waiting to be made better.

When I'm not buried in datasets or debugging models, I'm exploring ideas at the edge of what technology can do — curious, methodical, and occasionally too caffeinated.
